Open App View on GitHub

How it works

Create

Two independent key shares created together. No single private key ever exists.

Store

One share in your browser, one on the server. Both encrypted.

Sign

Both shares cooperate to sign. No single point of failure.

Features

2-of-2 MPC

Coinbase cb-mpc threshold crypto. No seed phrases.

Self-Custodial

Server never sees your full key. Export anytime.

Policy Engine

Spending limits, whitelists, 24h cooling.

Passkey Auth

Biometrics or anonymous login. No passwords.

Recovery Mode

Export both shares. Sign offline anytime.

Expert Mode

UTXO selection, RBF, nonce, sign-only, custom RPC, raw messages.

Why kexify

vs. MetaMask

Single key in browser. One breach = everything gone. kexify uses two independent key shares — no single point of failure.

vs. Fireblocks

Enterprise MPC, enterprise pricing. kexify brings the same threshold cryptography to individuals — free, no subscription, no ads.

vs. Custodial Wallets

Not your keys, not your crypto. kexify is self-custodial — export both shares and go fully offline anytime.
