How it works

1

Create

Generate a wallet. Your private key is split into two shares using MPC — no seed phrases.

2

Store

One share stays in your browser, the other on the server. Both encrypted. Neither is a full key.

3

Sign

Both shares cooperate to sign transactions. No single point of failure. You stay in control.

Features

2-of-2 MPC

Powered by Coinbase's cb-mpc threshold cryptography. No seed phrases ever.

Self-Custodial

Server never sees your full private key. Export your shares and go fully offline anytime.

Policy Engine

Spending limits, address whitelists, and 24-hour cooling periods. Your rules, enforced.

Passkey Auth

WebAuthn biometric authentication. No passwords to remember or leak.

Recovery Mode

Export both key shares. Sign offline if the server ever goes down. True self-custody.

WalletConnect

Connect to any dApp via WalletConnect v2. Sign transactions from your favorite protocols.

Why kexify

vs. MetaMask

Single key in browser. One breach = everything gone. kexify splits the key — no single point of failure.

vs. Fireblocks

Enterprise MPC, enterprise pricing. kexify brings the same threshold cryptography to individuals — free, no subscription, no ads.

vs. Custodial Wallets

Not your keys, not your crypto. kexify is self-custodial — export both shares and go fully offline anytime.
