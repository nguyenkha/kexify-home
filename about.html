About

kexify grew out of years building wallet and blockchain infrastructure at Quoine, Liquid, FTX Japan, and Custodiem. The lesson was clear: institutional-grade key management shouldn't require an institution. kexify brings 2-of-2 MPC signing to individuals — no seed phrases, no single point of failure, no compromise between convenience and security.

Designed for everyone, secured for real. Most MPC wallets are built for institutions — complex dashboards, approval workflows, enterprise pricing. kexify strips all that away. Sign in with email, approve with your fingerprint, send in three taps. Under the hood, the same threshold cryptography protects your keys. You don't need to understand MPC to benefit from it.

Philosophy

Simple

A wallet does one thing well — hold and sign. No token swaps, no staking, no DeFi built in. Keep the surface area small, keep the bugs out.

Just a wallet

Everything else — swaps, lending, bridges — is already solved by dApps like MyEtherWallet, 1inch, Aave, and hundreds more. Connect via WalletConnect and use the best tool for each job.

UX and security, nothing else

Every decision optimizes for two things: is it easy to use, and is it secure. No feature ships if it compromises either. Free forever because it was built for the author to use — not to monetize.
